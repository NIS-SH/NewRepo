Classic Vanilla Cake

Serves: 8 • Prep: 20 mins • Bake: 30–35 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla, then beat until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Let the cake cool for 10 minutes before removing it from the pan.
  6. Serve plain or with frosting, fruit, or whipped cream.

Tip: Do not overmix the batter, or the cake may become dense.
