Spicy Chicken Lollipop

Serves: 4 • Prep: 20 mins • Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken wings by removing the tips and shaping them into lollipops.
  2. Mix the ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, chilli sauce, pepper, and salt.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces and let them marinate for 15 minutes.
  4. Mix cornflour and flour, then coat the chicken evenly.
  5. Deep-fry until golden and crisp, then serve hot with sauce.

Tip: Fry on medium heat so the coating becomes crisp without burning.
