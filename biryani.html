Hyderabadi Biryani

Serves: 4 • Prep: 30 mins • Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or vegetables
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Fresh coriander and mint
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Cook the rice until it is 70% done and set it aside.
  2. Fry the onions until golden, then add the ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes.
  3. Add the meat or vegetables, spices, yogurt, and herbs, and cook until done.
  4. Layer the cooked rice over the masala and drizzle with saffron milk or ghee.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  6. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Tip: Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes after cooking for the best flavor.
